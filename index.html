About Dynamic Spectrum Access

Dynamic Spectrum Access (DSA) is a technology that allows efficient use of the radio frequency spectrum by dynamically allocating channels to users. This website demonstrates user management and real-time status tracking for DSA systems.

A cognitive radio is used to scan the 433MHz - 450MHz frequency band to determine and identify points on the frequency band that are open and allow users to opportunistically access the free bands.
